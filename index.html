1. Do solar panels require maintenance?

Solar panels are low-maintenance, but regular cleaning and inspections are recommended to ensure optimal performance. In most cases, rainwater is sufficient to keep panels clean. However, if needed, our team can provide guidance on maintenance best practices or assist you with professional cleaning services.


2. Are solar panels suitable for my home?

Absolutely! Solar panels can be installed on most residential rooftops. Our team of experts will assess your home's location, roof orientation, and other factors to determine the feasibility of solar panel installation. We'll work closely with you to design a system that maximizes energy production and fits seamlessly with your home's aesthetics.


3. Is financing available for solar panel installation?

Absolutely! We offer a range of financing options to make solar panel installation affordable for every homeowner. Our team will work with you to explore financing solutions such as solar loans, leasing, or power purchase agreements (PPAs) that best suit your budget and financial goals.


Will solar panels work during cloudy or rainy days?

Solar panels can still generate electricity during cloudy or rainy days, although their output may be reduced. Solar technology has advanced significantly, allowing panels to capture diffused sunlight and convert it into usable energy. Rest assured, your solar panels will continue to contribute to your energy needs even in less-than-optimal weather conditions.
